History

How did W4P start?

  • March 2014

    It all started with a common problem

    Open Knowledge Belgium and iDROPS met up to discuss overlapping projects and goals and quickly found a common problem, funding. It's really hard these days to fund relative small projects that only have local impact. We thought crowdsourcing could be an opportunity but social innovation projects are often fading on large crowdfunding websites.

  • September 2014

    An idea turned in a project proposal

    Six months later, we found the perfect project call. Chest project - Call3 was looking for Open Source innovation projects that transform social innovation. We submitted and hoped for the best.

    Project approved, time to work
